Privacy Policy for AfiaSam Manager

Last Updated: December 29, 2025

1. Introduction
AfiaSam Manager ("we", "our", or "us") provides a software solution developed to assist in the management of e-commerce operations. This Privacy Policy explains how our application handles data retrieved from the TikTok Shop Open Platform.

2. Data Collection
Our application accesses the following data from your TikTok Shop seller account via the official API:

Order Information: Order IDs, status, timestamps, and pricing details.

Product Information: SKU, product names, and inventory levels.

fulfillment Data: Shipping tracking numbers and carrier information.

3. Use of Data
The data collected is used solely for the following internal business purposes:

Generating sales reports and financial statements.

Automating invoice creation.

Synchronizing inventory across internal systems.

4. Data Sharing and Disclosure
We do not sell, trade, or transfer your data to outside parties. Data is processed locally within the application environment or stored in our secure internal databases for the purposes described above.

5. Data Retention
We retain order data only as long as necessary to fulfill legal, tax, and accounting requirements associated with our business operations.
